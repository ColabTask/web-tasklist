How much does this service cost ?

The Colabtask website is a free service for all basic projects, as well as the mobile application that is attached to it.

You can freely create an account, add projects and use them for as long as you want.

Non-basic projects can be paid for private enterprise projects to help finance the costs of maintaining our services and to improve the quality of our offer.
